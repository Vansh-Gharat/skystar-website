Who We Are

Skystar Multiglobal Services Pvt Ltd

Established in 2024, Skystar Multiglobal Services is a government-authorized logistics provider based in Mumbai, specializing in customs clearance, freight forwarding, and supply chain solutions near JNPT port.

We provide fast, cost-effective, and expert customs clearance services for both imports and exports via air and sea. Our extensive experience in Indian customs regulations enables us to handle complex documentation, regulatory compliance, and duty structures.

End-to-end logistics solutions under one roof
Strategic location near JNPT for faster clearance
Technology-driven tracking and automated reporting
Serving retail, e-commerce, and construction sectors
